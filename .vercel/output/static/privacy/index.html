Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 25, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to SideBuzCom ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website sidebuz.com.

2. Information We Collect

When you use our tools (such as the Student Income Finder), you may provide information including your field of study, available time, skills, and income goals. This information is processed locally in your browser and is not stored on our servers.

We may automatically collect certain information when you visit our website, including:

We use the information we collect to:

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

Our website is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our About page.
